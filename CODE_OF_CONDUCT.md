# Code Of Conduct

This project is for people learning and building together around open small language models.

Be kind, direct, and constructive. Treat contributors with respect regardless of background, identity, experience level, or technical choices.

Good project discussion should focus on:

- Whether a spec is clear and useful
- Whether examples match the stated behavior
- Whether dataset, model, and eval claims are accurate
- Whether safety and licensing notes are honest

Do not harass, threaten, demean, or exclude people. Do not post private information, credentials, or data you do not have permission to share.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that make the project unsafe or hostile.
